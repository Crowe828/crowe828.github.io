Me: An Introduction.

I'm Christian. A few years back I graduated from UCF with my Bachelors in Psychology. After a few years of working in sales and recruitment, I realized that it wasn't for me. I also realized that I've always loved technology. The idea of doing this every day is incredibly exciting to me! I can't wait to learn more.

Fun facts about me: I have a collection of over 50 Rubik's Cubes (or "geometric puzzles" as they should be called. I have a podcast called "Bad Movie Journalists" where we discuss the beauty in mediocre cinema. I love to travel and I've been to Japan twice, I'm convinced it's the best place on earth.

Some of my favorite movies are Scott Pilgrim vs. the World, Army of Darkness, and American Werewolf in London. Some of my favorite bands are Muse, Gorillaz, and Paramore. My favorite foods are sushi, ramen, and burritos.
